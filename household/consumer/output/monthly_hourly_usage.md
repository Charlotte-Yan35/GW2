# Monthly 24-Hour Household Electricity Usage

## Data & Method

- **Source**: London Smart Meter dataset (`Small_LCL_Data.parquet`), ~5 500 households, 2011–2014
- **Granularity**: Half-hourly readings summed to hourly kWh
- **Aggregation**: Per household × month × hour mean → cross-household **mean**, **P10**, **P90**
- P10–P90 range captures the spread across households
